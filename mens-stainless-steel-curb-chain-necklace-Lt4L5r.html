Higher karat gold offers a richer yellow hue, while lower karats are more durable and often more affordable.

Beyond purity, gold chains come in different colors: classic yellow gold, modern white gold (alloyed with palladium or nickel and plated with rhodium), and romantic rose gold (alloyed with copper). Each offers a unique aesthetic, allowing for diverse expressions of **neckwear**.
